# MINIMALS - Safari Feeling for Your Home

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Important Note:** This project is about making the "Minimals" concept (miniature wild animals as pets) tangible. There is no company behind it; this site exists solely to make this vision accessible.


**🌐 [View Live Demo](https://xerxesr.github.io/Minimals/)**

## Project Overview
MINIMALS is a fictional website for ethically developed mini wild animals as pets. The concept is based on the vision of downsizing wild animals like elephants, giraffes, and zebras to pet size (approx. 20cm) – perfect for modern city apartments.

This project serves as a demonstration for:
- The **Minimals** concept – the vision of downsized wild animals as pets.
- Integration of AI-generated, photorealistic media.
- Multilingual support (i18n) and responsive design.

## Features
- **3-Page Structure:** Vision (Storytelling), Animals Overview (Products), Habitats Overview (Accessories).
- **Fully Bilingual:** Seamless language switching between German and English.
- **Interactive UI:** Shopping cart simulation, responsive navigation, and smooth animations.
- **Design System:** A warm, African savanna color palette for an authentic experience.

## Story & Vision
Founded by biotechnologist Dr. Sarah Nakamura, MINIMALS pursues the mission of bringing the beauty of the wilderness directly into our homes while simultaneously raising awareness for wildlife conservation.

### Ethics & Mission
- **Conservation:** 30% of all fictional sales proceeds go to wildlife protection organizations.
- **Responsibility:** Sterile breeding prevents uncontrolled reproduction in foreign ecosystems.
- **Education:** Promoting respect for wildlife through direct interaction.

## Technology & Implementation
- **Framework:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript (no external libraries).
- **Images:** All visual materials were generated with state-of-the-art AI to ensure a consistent and high-quality aesthetic. The corresponding prompts can be found in the `images/prompts/` folder.
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first design for optimal display on all devices.
